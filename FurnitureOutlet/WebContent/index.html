Mission Statement:

To be Wisconsin's best priced variety furniture store!

SMALL BUSINESS SURVIVES!


Open 7 days a week, family owned Black River Furniture Outlet has been offering furniture in some way for the last 20 years. We started out with a small section of furniture in our Black River Surplus store and have grown to our new location of over 20,000 square feet of upholstery, mattresses, dining, entertainment, bedroom, cabin, occasional and home office furniture. And we have great deals on rugs too!
When it comes to upholstery, we try to have 50 – 100 recliners on hand at all times. We have racks of sofas and sectionals set up for our customers. Our sleep center offers 25 different styles of mattresses. In our dining section we stock drop leaf tables to major dining sets both regular and counter height. We always have bedroom sets and individual bedroom pieces available. We usually have a reasonable variety of closeouts and stock items when it comes to entertainment, home office and coffee table sets. The majority of rugs we stock are from Dalton Georgia and they are always priced right.
Our cabin furniture area offers aspen, hickory, pine and northern cedar products along with a custom ordering upholstery program. It's working out great for our customers because they can get what they want at reasonable prices. Nice cabin furniture is expensive, but it doesn't have to be as expensive as what our customers have told us about and what we've seen. Currently we are committing to a modest interstate sign that will read “Cabin Furniture Outlet” on I94. If you've seen this sign and you need cabin furniture please stop by.
We are not a high pressure furniture store. We are a customer oriented store that recognizes that you might want to purchase today or 6 months from today or whenever. Our goal is to give you information, product selection, good pricing and to build a business relationship. Our products change weekly as we are a volume business continuing to build variety and opportunity for our customers.
Welcome & be invited if you live in Black River Falls or the surrounding area, or if you are passing through, to shop Black River Furniture Outlet! Open 7 days a week. Hwy 54 “On The East Side!”
